📊 SUPERSTORE SALES ANALYSIS - KEY INSIGHTS



1. DATA QUALITY ASSESSMENT

• Dataset contains 9,994 retail transaction records.
• No missing values were found in key columns after validation.
• 17 duplicate records were identified using the ROW_NUMBER() window function.
• Sales, Quantity, and Discount fields contain no invalid negative values.
• Profit contains both positive and negative values, representing profitable and loss-making transactions.



2. DATA OVERVIEW

• The dataset contains retail sales transactions across the United States.
• Three product categories are present:
  - Technology
  - Furniture
  - Office Supplies

• Sales are distributed across four regions:
  - West
  - East
  - Central
  - South



3. CATEGORY-WISE SALES ANALYSIS

Technology generated the highest revenue:

• Technology: $836,154.03
• Furniture: $741,999.80
• Office Supplies: $719,047.03

Insight:
Technology is the leading category in terms of total sales contribution.



4. CATEGORY-WISE PROFIT ANALYSIS

• Technology generated the highest overall profit.
• Office Supplies contributed strong profitability.
• Furniture generated the lowest profit despite substantial sales.

Insight:
Higher sales do not always translate into higher profit. Furniture shows comparatively weaker profitability due to discounting or cost structure.


5. REGION-WISE SALES ANALYSIS

• West region recorded the highest sales performance.
• East region ranked second.
• Central region showed moderate sales performance.
• South region generated the lowest sales.

Insight:
Sales performance varies significantly across regions, indicating regional demand differences.



6. TOP PROFITABLE STATES

Top-performing states based on total profit:

1. California ($76,381.39)
2. New York ($74,038.55)
3. Washington ($33,402.65)
4. Michigan ($24,463.19)
5. Virginia ($18,597.95)

Insight:
California and New York are the most profitable states and contribute significantly to overall business profitability.


7. LOSS-MAKING STATES

Several states generated negative overall profit despite recording sales activity:

• Texas
• Ohio
• Pennsylvania
• Illinois
• North Carolina
• Colorado
• Tennessee
• Arizona
• Florida
• Oregon

Insight:
High sales volume alone does not guarantee profitability. These states may require improvements in pricing strategy, discount control, or operational efficiency.



8. BUSINESS INSIGHTS

• Technology is the strongest category in both sales and profit.
• Furniture shows relatively high sales but significantly lower profitability, indicating potential issues with discounting or cost structure.
• West region contributes the highest revenue.
• South region represents the weakest sales-performing region.
• Multiple states are loss-making despite active sales.
• California and New York are major profit-driving markets.
